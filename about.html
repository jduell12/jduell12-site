Hello! My name is Jessica and I am currently a student at Oregon State University studying Computer Science. I first got interested in becoming a software developer when a friend asked me to take a free bootcamp with him. I fell in love with developing software every since!

After getting my feet wet with the bootcamp I decided to pursue some college courses at my local community college while working. I completed my Associate's degree from Washtenaw Community College in Computer Science: Programming in Java. After I completed the Associate's degree I decided I wanted a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. Since I needed to work while going to school I chose Oregon State Univeristy's online program.

The main languages I enjoy working in is C++ and Java. I am working on honing my skills in Python as well as JavaScript. I have experience working with Mac and Microsoft systems as well as the Linux environment.
